About Holy Paladin

Holy Paladins are healers that gain strength through the power of the Light, Holy Paladins use powerful healing spells, blessings, and auras to keep themselves and their allies healed through dire situations. While bringing the fight to their enemies damaging them with Holy Light. Holy paladins specifically focus on Healing, as such in World of Warcraft they fit into the role of Healer. Wheras Retribution focuses on damage and Protection focuses on protecting allies from harm. Your role is to keep your party or raid alive through the toughest of times. Holy Paladins are among the most desired class in the game due to their large amount of support abilities being an asset to any raid or party. Whether you are solo questing, world first raiding, high-end raiding or the occasional mythic+ you won't have trouble finding a group that needs a paladin!

What seperates Holy Paladins from other healers?

Holy paladins specialize in single target healing. Wheras most other healers have a large tool of abilities to heal large groups of allies at the same time, Paladins heal a small group of players for a large amount. This means they excel at saving people who need the most amount of healing at that time. So the concept of Triage is important as a paladin. Triage is simply put the idea that you should be focusing your efforts on the person who needs the most amount of help and then work your way up from most injured -> least injured. As such practice makes perfect as a Paladin. The best way to learn is to keep playing and getting into situations where you are in over your head a bit, to get a feel for the encounter and the other classes so you can learn through experience who is in most danger at different times of an encounter, dungeon or raid.

The Battle Healer

Paladins however are somewhat unique. The core fantasy of the Paladin involves being on the front-lines with your allies protecting them from harm and dealing some damage yourself. The gameplay also supports this with a series of talents and abilities that incentivize you to be in melee weaving in damage spells while healing your allies. For more information about these play styles check out our talents page. Even without taking on a series of talents that incentivize you to do damage. Paladins bring the largest amount of raw damage output of any healer in terms of DPS(Damage per second). This means while taking on a full build maximized for healing you should be doing some damage as there will be times when your party is safe and you are best off dealing some damage during that 'down-time".
