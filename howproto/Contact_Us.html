Log analysis!

You can also submit a Warcraftlogs log to be analyzed by us. We sometimes get quite a few of these, so it may take some time for us to get back to you. However feel free to send it in and someone will email you with feedback on your performance and ways to improve! This service is completely free and done by players looking to help in their spare time so there is no ETA for when someone will get back to you.
